About Me

I’ve always been fascinated by how things work, and from an early age, I found myself drawn to the world of technology.   • Machine Learning Enthusiast: My curiosity extends to the world of AI and machine learning. I’m constantly exploring how intelligent systems can be used to solve real-world problems and make processes more efficient.

My Journey:

Growing up in Kolkata, I was always surrounded by vibrant culture and rich tradition, which sparked my curiosity and love for learning. Being a curious cat I explore new topics and often take interest in a variety of subjects.

My journey hasn’t just been about technology, though. I’ve also been fortunate to capture the beauty of nature, with Kolkata holding a special place in my heart for its diverse culture. I like to think of it as my creative retreat—whenever I need inspiration, I grab my camera and get lost in the old streets of Kolkata with the same old yellow taxis and street vendors
